## Setup Guide

Naridon is a Shopify App that integrates directly with your store. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the App">
    Go to the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/naridon) and click **Install**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    Approve the necessary permissions for Naridon to access your product data and theme. 
    
    *Note: We need these permissions to analyze your products for AI optimization and to display the Copilot widget on your storefront.*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboarding & Analysis">
    Once installed, Naridon will automatically start an initial scan of your store. 
    
    This process:
    - Analyzes your current product schema
    - Checks for visibility on major AI platforms
    - Identifies immediate optimization opportunities
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Copilot (Optional)">
    Navigate to the **Copilot** tab in the Naridon dashboard to customize and enable the storefront sales agent.
    
    You can:
    - Customize the widget color and branding
    - Set the welcome message
    - Toggle visibility rules
  </Step>
</Steps>
