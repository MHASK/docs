## 1. Introduction
This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) outlines the prohibited uses of the Naridon platform. By using our services, you agree to comply with this policy.

## 2. Prohibited Content
You may not use Naridon to generate, host, or promote content that:
- Is illegal, harmful, or fraudulent.
- Infringes on the intellectual property rights of others.
- Contains hate speech, harassment, or threats of violence.
- Promotes self-harm or illegal acts.
- Contains sexually explicit material or child exploitation.

## 3. Prohibited Actions
You agree not to:
- **Reverse Engineer**: Attempt to reverse engineer, decompile, or discover the source code or underlying algorithms of the Service.
- **Scrape**: Use automated systems (bots, spiders) to scrape data from our platform without permission.
- **Interfere**: Disrupt the integrity or performance of the Service or third-party data contained therein.
- **Bypass**: Attempt to bypass or circumvent any security measures or usage limits (e.g., API rate limits).

## 4. AI Interaction Standards
When using our AI features, you must not:
- Use the AI to generate misleading or deceptive content.
- Attempt to "jailbreak" or override the safety guardrails of the underlying AI models.
- Use the AI to provide medical, legal, or financial advice where professional certification is required.

## 5. Enforcement
We reserve the right to investigate any violation of this policy. If we determine that you have violated this AUP, we may:
- Remove offending content.
- Suspend or terminate your access to the Service.
- Report unlawful conduct to law enforcement authorities.

## 6. Reporting Violations
If you become aware of any violation of this policy, please report it to us immediately at [support@naridon.com](mailto:support@naridon.com).
