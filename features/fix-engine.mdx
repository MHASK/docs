## Automated Health Checks

The **Auto-Pilot Fix Engine** is your store's 24/7 mechanic. It performs daily automated scans to ensure your data meets the strict standards of AI models.

## Key Functions

### 1. Schema Repair
Naridon automatically identifies and fixes structured data issues to ensure AI models can properly understand your products.

### 2. Content Enrichment
Naridon detects products that need better descriptions and helps you create AI-optimized content.

## Configuration

You can configure how the Fix Engine operates in the **[Configuration](/features/settings)** menu, choosing between manual review or automated fixes.
