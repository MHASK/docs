## ROI Calculator

Understanding the return on investment from AI optimization can be tricky. Naridon's **ROI Calculator** estimates the revenue uplift generated specifically from AI-referred traffic.

## Key Metrics

### Traffic Attribution
Traditional analytics often lump AI traffic into "Direct" or "Referral." Naridon uses advanced tracking to identify:
- **Source**: ChatGPT, Bing Chat, Perplexity, Claude, etc.
- **Volume**: Number of visits from each AI platform.
- **Trend**: Growth of AI traffic over time.

### Engagement Metrics
See how users are interacting with your content.
- **Top Questions**: The most common questions customers are asking (invaluable for product development and marketing).

### Visibility Tracking
Monitor your **Visibility Score** across different AI models.
- **Share of Voice**: How often your brand appears compared to competitors for your target prompts.
- **Citation Frequency**: How often your store is cited as a source in AI answers.
