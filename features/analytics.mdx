## ROI Calculator

Understanding the return on investment from AI optimization is crucial. Naridon's new **ROI Calculator** estimates the revenue uplift generated specifically from AI-referred traffic.

We analyze the traffic sources identified as "AI Chatbots" and apply an estimated conversion rate to calculate your potential revenue gain from improved visibility.

## Key Metrics

### 1. Share of Voice
This is your primary competitive metric. It represents the percentage of the market (tracked prompts) where your brand appears in the conversation compared to your competitors.

### 2. Product Gaps
A health metric showing the number of products in your store that are missing critical metadata:
- Missing Descriptions
- Missing Alt Text
- Invalid Schema

### 3. Traffic Attribution
Naridon uses advanced tracking to identify visits from:
- **DeepSeek**
- **ChatGPT**
- **Perplexity**
- **Claude**
