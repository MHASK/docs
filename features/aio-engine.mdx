## What is the AIO Engine?

The **AIO (AI Search Optimization) Engine** is the core of Naridon. It ensures your products are discovered, understood, and recommended by AI models like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini.

Just as SEO optimizes for Google's algorithm, AIO optimizes for Large Language Models (LLMs).

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Data Optimization
Naridon builds a comprehensive data layer for your store to ensure AI models understand your products.

### 2. Performance Tracking
Monitor how your brand appears across different AI platforms and track improvements over time.

### 3. Query Analysis
Track how your products perform for different types of customer queries and search patterns.

### 4. Competitive Insights
Understand how you compare to competitors in AI search results.

## How It Works

Naridon continuously analyzes your store data, optimizes it for AI models, and monitors your performance to ensure your products are discoverable in AI conversations.
