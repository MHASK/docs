## What is the AIO Engine?

The **AIO (AI Search Optimization) Engine** is the core of Naridon. It ensures your products are discovered, understood, and recommended by modern AI models.

## Supported AI Models

Naridon optimizes your visibility across the leading AI platforms. We actively track and emulate:
- **OpenAI**: ChatGPT (GPT-4o)
- **Anthropic**: Claude (3.5 Sonnet)
- **Google**: Gemini (2.0 Flash)
- **Perplexity**: Online Search
- **Meta**: Llama 3
- **DeepSeek**: DeepSeek V3 Chat

## Global Visibility Score

Your **Global Visibility Score (0-100)** is the primary metric for your store's AI health. It is calculated based on:
1.  **Top 5 Appearance**: The percentage of your tracked prompts where your brand appears in the top 5 suggestions.
2.  **Share of Voice**: Your market presence compared to tracked competitors.

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Advanced Data Structuring
Naridon builds a rich, structured data layer for your store. We now index **Products**, **Collections**, and **Blog Posts** to give AI models a complete context of your business.

### 2. Prompt Monitoring
Track specific, high-intent user queries.
- **Silent Scan**: During onboarding, we automatically identify high-value prompts relevant to your niche.
- **Analysis Runs**: We use **DeepSeek V3** and **Gemini 2.0 Flash** to perform cost-effective, high-frequency analysis of your ranking.

### 3. Competitor Intelligence
Analyze how your competitors are being recommended.
- **Benchmarking**: Compare your Share of Voice against key competitors.
- **Strategy Adjustments**: Adjust your store's data signals to outrank competitors.
