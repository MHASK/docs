## AIO Controls

Manage how Naridon interacts with your store data and how autonomously it operates.

### Automation Settings
Configure how Naridon scans and optimizes your store automatically.

### Safety & Limits
Control the level of automation and set limits on how many changes can be made.

### Permissions
Manage what types of content Naridon can automatically update.

## AI Sitemap

Naridon generates specialized files to help AI models understand your store structure and content.

### Configuration
Configure what content is included and add custom guidelines for AI models.

### Why this matters
Proper configuration helps ensure AI models can correctly index and reference your store information.
